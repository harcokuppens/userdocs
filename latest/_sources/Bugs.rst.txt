Bugs\ `¶ <#Bugs>`__
===================

| When you find a bug in TorXakis, please send a bug report to
  jan.tretmans AT tno.nl and pierre.vandelaar AT tno.nl.
| For hints on writing a good bug report, look at `Apache’s bug writing
  guidelines <https://issues.apache.org/bugwritinghelp.html>`__ and
  `Mozilla’s bug writing
  guidelines <https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Mozilla/QA/Bug_writing_guidelines>`__.
